THE KITTEN CARTER

JANUARY 26, 2024, WAS ONE OF THE GREATEST DAYS I EVER HAD. HEY, IT’S ME, KHALIIL, FROM KHALIIIL.COM. I AM SOMEONE WHO NEVER

FOUND A REAL CLOSE FRIEND. I DON’T HAVE ANY BROTHERS OR SISTERS, BUT I WISH I HAD… IN THIS BLOG, I DON’T WANT TO TALK
ABOUT MY FEELINGS BEING ALONE. JANUARY 26 WAS THE DREAM DAY BECAUSE, ON THAT
DAY, I FOUND THE FRIEND I HAD BEEN LOOKING
FOR. I GOT CARTER, MY NEW KITTEN—MY FIRST AND LAST BEST FRIEND, BROTHER, SISTER, AND EVEN MY SON. TODAY, MAY 20, 2024, I CAN SAY THAT I
AM COMPLETELY WEAK IN FRONT OF HIM AND CANNOT LIVE ONE DAY WITHOUT SEEING HIM. HE IS A
PIECE OF ME. THE FEELING OF HAVING THE ONE I HAD BEEN SEARCHING FOR A VERY LONG TIME ALWAYS NEXT TO ME IS COMPLETELY DIFFERENT!
THE FEELING OF HAVING THE BROTHER OR SISTER I ALWAYS WISHED FOR WITH ME IS ANOTHER LIFE. I CAN SAY NOW THAT CARTER WILL FILL ALL THE GAPS I
HAD IN MY LIFE. FINALLY, I HAVE MY OWN KITTEN, WHICH I NEVER THOUGHT WOULD HELP ME OVERCOME THE HUMILIATION AND OPPRESSION CAUSED BY OTHERS. PEOPLE AND FRIENDS THINK
THAT IF THEY MAKE A VERY SMALL MISTAKE WITH THEIR FRIENDS, IT WILL BE OKAY AND WON’T AFFECT THEIR FEELINGS. THEY MIGHT NOT SHOW WHAT THEY FEEL, BUT YOU HAVE TO KNOW THAT IT WILL ALL BE INSIDE

THEIR HEARTS! SO BE AWARE OF WHAT YOU SAY. LAST UPDATED: JUNE 13, 2024
