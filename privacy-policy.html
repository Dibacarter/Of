THIS PRIVACY POLICY EXPLAINS HOW KHALIIIL.COM COLLECTS AND USES PERSONAL INFORMATION WHEN YOU VISIT OUR WEBSITE.

THE NAME PROVIDED IS SOLELY USED FOR IDENTIFICATION PURPOSES TO GRANT ACCESS TO THE PRIVATE PAGE. WE DO NOT SHARE THIS INFORMATION WITH ANY THIRD PARTIES.

WE TAKE APPROPRIATE MEASURES TO ENSURE THE SECURITY OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION. ACCESS TO THE PRIVATE PAGE IS PROTECTED BY A PASSWORD, AND WE REGULARLY REVIEW OUR SECURITY PROTOCOLS TO SAFEGUARD YOUR DATA.
